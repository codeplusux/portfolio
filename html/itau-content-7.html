Impact

  • ~90% drop in support calls related to card blocks.
  • ~$81,000 saved in support costs over 6 months
  • Feature adopted by 50,000+ users in the first semester.
  • Improved customer perception of security (confirmed by NPS boost in post-interaction surveys).

Key Learnings

  • Clarity > speed: Users preferred a 2-step process with clear status feedback over a faster but ambiguous flow.
  • Stakeholder alignment early on made implementation smoother and more secure
  • Microcopy and transitions played a critical role in building user trust in moments of stress.
